About

General Focus

Being an out-state-student to a new school can be very stressful when you don’t know the Rochester area very well. Out-of-state students and especially international students might have a hard time finding housing, transportation, and getting used to the area. Organizations exist at RIT to assist these students with these problems but they might have a tough time reaching all of the students that may need their help.

We want to make it easier for existing RIT students who wish to help new students to do so. Our project will focus on building a bridge between these two groups of students so that the new students can be assisted and have their questions answered even before classes start. Our main demographic focus will be on international and transfer students who are living off campus but the project could also assist freshmen living in dorms as well.

The main focus of the website itself will be to help students find housing in the area and issues that relate to that such as transportation from a certain area, stores in a certain area etc. Through our interviews we want to see if there are more important issues that students would like help finding out about.

Target Audience

Our customer, stakeholders, and users

Customers

We do not have a customer at this time but would be interested in talking to an RIT organization that might find this product useful.

Stakeholders

RIT Students especially students having trouble finding housing, The Team, Housing Administration Companies/Landlords, RIT organizations that help international and out-of-state students

Users

RIT Students, Housing Administration, Someone looking for Housing
