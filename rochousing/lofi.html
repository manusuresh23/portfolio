About

General Focus

Being an out-state-student to a new school can be very stressful when you don’t know the Rochester area very well. Out-of-state students and especially international students might have a hard time finding housing, transportation, and getting used to the area. Organizations exist at RIT to assist these students with these problems but they might have a tough time reaching all of the students that may need their help.

We want to make it easier for existing RIT students who wish to help new students to do so. Our project will focus on building a bridge between these two groups of students so that the new students can be assisted and have their questions answered even before classes start. Our main demographic focus will be on international and transfer students who are living off campus but the project could also assist freshmen living in dorms as well.

The main focus of the website itself will be to help students find housing in the area and issues that relate to that such as transportation from a certain area, stores in a certain area etc. Through our interviews we want to see if there are more important issues that students would like help finding out about.

Target Audience

Our customer, stakeholders, and users

Customers

We do not have a customer at this time but would be interested in talking to an RIT organization that might find this product useful.

Stakeholders

RIT Students especially students having trouble finding housing, The Team, Housing Administration Companies/Landlords, RIT organizations that help international and out-of-state students

Users

RIT Students, Housing Administration, Someone looking for Housing

LoFi Prototype

Here are our lofi prototype pictures

HOMEPAGE - About - Screen 1.1

This screen contains the name of the website, punchline. The page is embedded with the image and information about the website. Other organizations including ISS that are helping with student housing are introduced. To know more about the organizations, the user will be led to the “Organization Information”- Screen 1.3. Moreover, the GIF of down arrow signifies to scroll down to navigate to the other section of the page. It also has three tabs on the top: About, Map and Student Employees which are the sections of the homepage. The ISS hyperlink on the top will direct user to ISS's website.


HOMEPAGE - Map - Screen 1.2

The other section of the homepage contains a map, that shows the housings and its proximity to RIT. User can hover over the icons of the houses and see the information when it expands. To view more information user would click on it. The other way to look for houses is clicking the ‘Filter’ tab on top of the map, which leads to “Housing Filter”- Screen 2.1. And upon clicking the housing icons, user will be navigated to “Housing Information”- Screen 2.2.







HOMEPAGE- Student Employees - Screen 1.3

Here is the information of the student employees hired by ISS to help out the incoming International Students. The search option gives user opportunity to find out the student employees they know.


HOUSING FILTER - Screen 2.1

With Likert scale and slide, filtering is easy and interactive. After entering the query user will go back to the homepage- Screen 1.2 and will see the housing options according to the filtering query entered by user.


HOUSING INFORMATION - Screen 2.2

User will be navigated here upon clicking a house in the map of “Homepage”- Screen 1.2. The information of that particular housing has been rendered. The ‘image’ section will contain different views of the house. Moreover, the transportation facilities from the closest bus stops to the house, recreational and shopping places are highlighted. For a detailed view users will have to click ‘More’ which will direct them to expanded version to “Transportation”- Screen 2.2.1, “Recreation”- Screen 2.2.2, and “Shopping”- Screen 2.2.3.


TRANSPORTATION- Screen 2.2.1

This screen is a more detailed view of one of the vertical tabs on housing info page, transportation. A bus schedule/ stops within the proximity of the selected housing option will be displayed.













RECREATION- Screen 2.2.2

This screen is a more detailed view of one of the other vertical tabs on housing info page, recreation. Recreation options closer to the selected housing will be displayed organized by miles from the selected housing..

SHOPPING- Screen 2.2.3

This screen is a more detailed view of one of the other vertical tabs on housing info page, shopping. Shopping options closer to the selected housing will be displayed organized by miles from the selected housing.




CHAT 1

This page explains the login flow whether the user is able to successfully login or not. The login is only to use the chat functionality in order to avoid any spam messages. Login for existing students who are part of some of the organizations involved with this website is also explained here.

CHAT 2

This login screen explains login instructions and also link to RIT page with info on how to get RIT username and password.























FAQ 1

The list of basic questions are listed here so user can decide if they want to talk to student employees for any more information!



FAQ 2

Every question is a hyperlink. User can click on one of them and the answer will be displayed.
